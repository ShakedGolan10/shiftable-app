How will it look:
    A table of 7 days, in each cube (cell) will be a shift
    The number of cubes and cells will be determine by the Employer settings (based on the shifts in that correct job)
    As an Employee you can check the cells (shifts) you can work on based on the Employer defenitions and also mark specific Shifts you cant

    The Shifts that the Employee want to work at would be marked in green
    Those he cant will be marked in red

    At the end of marking the Employee would need to confirm his choices,
    and If he doesnt stands up to the requirments he would be notified and couldnt complete

Technologies and Libraries:
    NextUI: 
        - Built on top of Tailwind css
        - Effortless dark mode
        - Ready components
        - Compability with NextJS

    Socket.io:
        - fast syncronize between employees and availble (not taken) shifts
        - Realtime notifications to Employer on shifts application did by employees
